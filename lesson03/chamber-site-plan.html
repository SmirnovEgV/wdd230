Overview

Purpose

The Chamber of Commerce of Minsk, Belarus, is dedicated to fostering economic growth, prosperity, cooperation within the Minsk and it surroundong area. Our mission is to provide comprehensive support, resources, and advocacy for local businesses, empowering them to thrive in a regional marketplace. We facilitate networking, promote investment opportunities, and serve as a bridge between businesses, government, and the community, all while championing the interests and economic development of Minsk and its surrounding areas.

  • What new policies were applied by the government?

  • How recent change of policies might affect my business?
